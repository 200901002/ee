Transistor Transfer + resistance https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CkX8SkTgB0g Thyratron Derived from ancient Greek Thyra - Door Thyratron is a gas-filled tube used as a high-power electrical switch. Thyristor This word is a combination of Thyratron + Transistor. What is meant by high input impedance? High impedance means that a point in a circuit (a node) allows a relatively small amount of current through, per unit of applied voltage at that point. Why an op amp should have high input impedance? - Because they are voltage-gain (devices that amplify voltages). In order to drop entire voltage across the input, the impedance has to be very high, as ohm's law states, V=IR. https://electronics.stackexchange.com/a/33802 - It's also important to prevent the loading effect. If the impedance were small, the current draw would be high which loads the source. Why ω is 2πf? https://drive.google.com/file/d/1mwj_DQP54Fdmz_frQmKZ8TkTHCFpvRxq/view?usp=sharing (edited)
